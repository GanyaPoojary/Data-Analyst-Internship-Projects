# 📊 Data Analytics Internship Projects

This repository contains projects completed during my Data Analyst Internship.  
These projects focus on solving real-world business problems using data analysis, visualization, and dashboard development.

---

## 📁 Projects Included

### 🔹 1. Superstore Sales Dashboard
Analyzed retail sales data to understand profit trends, discount impact, and regional performance.

**Key Insight:**  
High discount levels negatively impact profit margins, highlighting the need for optimized pricing strategies.

---

### 🔹 2. HR Attrition Analysis
Explored employee data to identify patterns behind workforce attrition and retention.

**Key Insight:**  
Employees are most likely to leave within their first 2 years, indicating the importance of early-stage engagement.

---

### 🔹 3. IPL Performance Analysis
Analyzed IPL cricket data to uncover insights about team performance, player statistics, and match outcomes.

**Key Insight:**  
Teams winning the toss have a slight advantage, and most prefer fielding first.
